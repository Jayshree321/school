Welcome to Saraswati Sishu Vidya Mandir

"Saraswati Shishu Vidya Mandir, Bramahni vihar,Talcher SARASWATI SHISHU VIDYA MANDIR is a co-educational Odia medium school affiliated to the Sikha Bikash Samiti,Bhubaneswar and is situated at Bhubaneswar of Talcher, serene and beautiful surrounding. The school aims at giving a qualitative high standard of education which would develop the students innate potential to the fullest. It is managed by Bal Sikhya Bikash Samiti , a registered body under the India Societies Registration Act of 1960 with eminent educationists and philanthropists. The School imparts education from Nursery to 10+2 level. Near about 2000 students study here for their all round development under the healthy guidance of able and experienced ACHARYAS. The School is affiliated to CHSE. The divine "Panchpadi teaching method" has been adopted to make the atmosphere of the school full of SANSKARS. ... more araswati Shishu Vidya Mandir,Talcher is a Co- Educational Senior Secondary School, with approximately 1000 Students on its rolls. These Children, in the Junior and Senior branches. The School is among the most distinguished members of Sikhya Bikash Samiti,Talcher. The School considers education to be a Life–long process which should have a strong foundation. The goal of the School is to inculcate in the students a love for learning and a desire to excel at every level .The school also aims at equipping the students with the intellectual and practical skills that are necessary to meet the challenges in the future. To sum up, the mission of Saraswati Shishu Vidya Mandir,Talcher is “To open doors and open minds” and prepare the ground for the future of the nation..
